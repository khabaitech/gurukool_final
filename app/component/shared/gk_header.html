FAQ
What is Gurukool?
Gurukool is a virtual alternative to traditional home or coaching classes based "tuiring".TutorReal Guru, on Online Tutoring provider replicates traditional face to face coaching with live Online and interactive tutoring incorporating state of the art virtual methods of teaching namely- interactive whiteboards,live video chats, interaction, and sharing of teaching and learning tools and resources.
